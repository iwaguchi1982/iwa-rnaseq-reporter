# Walkthrough - v0.20.4 Triage / Review Annotation

This milestone introduces the human-in-the-loop triage capability. Researchers can now record their manual review results, justify decisions with notes, and track overall review progress within the workspace.

## Key Accomplishments

### 1. Reviewer Annotation Layer
Introduced a dedicated layer for human annotations:
- **Triage Status**: Track progress with statuses like `Unreviewed`, `Flagged`, `Reviewed`, or `Handoff Candidate`.
- **Priority & Follow-up**: Mark critical comparisons for urgent attention or further discussion.
- **Review Notes**: Persistent text area for recording justifications or research context.

### 2. Progress Dashboard
The Review Workspace header now includes a **Review Progress & Annotation Summary**:
- Real-time counters showing how many comparisons have been reviewed or flagged.
- Clear distinction between "Consensus Metrics" (automated) and "Review Metrics" (human).

### 3. Integrated Triage Form
Added a user-friendly form to the "Inspection & Drilldown" pane:
- **Smart Form**: Pre-populates with existing annotations for the selected comparison.
- **Save/Clear Actions**: One-click updates with immediate session-state synchronization.
- **Read-only Snapshots**: Shows current annotation summary before the form for quick verification.

### 4. Session-Aware Persistence
- The `AnnotationStore` is strictly tied to the `source_consensus_run_id`.
- Successfully prevents cross-annotation between different consensus bundles while maintaining data during active review.
